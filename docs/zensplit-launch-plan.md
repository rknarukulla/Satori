## Strategy at a Glance

| Signal | Choice | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Day 1 format | Reel | Reels reach non-followers — Statics don't. Zero followers means zero organic reach on a Static. |
| Day 1 post | Post 4 — WhatsApp Bill War | Highest virality score. Universally relatable. Tag-worthy. |
| Posting frequency | Every 2 days (Days 1–17) | Business account recommendation. Quality > volume at cold start. |
| Posting time — Reels | 7 PM IST | Peak scroll time. Highest reach window. |
| Posting time — Carousels | 12 PM IST | Lunchtime saves. Highest save rate. |
| Posting time — Static | 9 AM IST | Morning scroll. Grid anchor, not opener. |
| Primary metric | Saves + shares | Instagram's strongest algorithmic signals at cold start. |
| Content mix | 4 Reels · 3 Carousels · 2 Static | Right ratio for Instagram 2026. |

**Brand voice:** Warm · Witty · Relatable · Never preachy. Like a smart friend who handles money without making it weird.
